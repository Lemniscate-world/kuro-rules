# RULE 75: Deep Desk Research - Full Detail

## The 5 Mandatory Dimensions
| # | Dimension | Minimum Evidence |
|---|-----------|-----------------|
| 1 | Personas | 3-4 personas with 2+ verbatim quotes each (Tier 1 sources) |
| 2 | Competitors | Feature matrix + pricing + UX gaps, 5+ competitors |
| 3 | Market Size | TAM/SAM/SOM with calculations, 3+ sources |
| 4 | Risk Analysis | 5 risks with evidence + remedies |
| 5 | Gap Analysis | 3+ gaps with proof of unmet need |

## Phase 1: Personas (45min)
Search Reddit/HN by segment. Extract verbatim quotes.
Document per persona: budget, pain frequency, current workaround, willingness to switch.
Deliverable: persona cards in validation_evidence.md

## Phase 2: Competitors (60min)
Feature matrix (yours vs 5+ competitors). Pricing tiers. Hidden costs.
UX gaps: 3+ user complaints per competitor from Reddit/HN.
Deliverable: feature_matrix.md

## Phase 3: Market Sizing (30min)
TAM = total industry (report-based)
SAM = targetable segment (calculate %)
SOM = obtainable year 1-3 (conservative)
Deliverable: market_sizing.md

## Phase 4: Risk Analysis (30min)
5 mandatory risks: Market, Competition, Technical, Regulatory, Adoption
Template per risk: Probability | Impact | Evidence For | Evidence Against | Remedy
Deliverable: risk_analysis.md (see also R14.5)

## Phase 5: Gap Analysis (15min)
What do users complain about in ALL existing solutions?
What feature is mentioned but never built?
What workarounds are people cobbling together?
Deliverable: 3-5 gaps in validation_evidence.md

## Quality Thresholds
GO: 3+ personas | 5+ competitors | TAM/SAM/SOM sourced | 5 risks | 3+ gaps
STOP RED FLAGS: Cannot find 3 personas | No competitor >10K users | All gaps tried+failed

## Enforcement
Shallow research (< 3 dimensions) = STOP. No MVP. No GO decision.
5-dimension complete = OK to proceed. Document in desk_research_report.md
