## Quick Reference: Mom Test Rules

1. **Don't talk about your idea** — Talk about the problem only
2. **Past, not future** — Ask what happened, not what would happen
3. **Listen > Talk** — 25% talking, 75% listening

### Positive Signals (Continue)
- "I spent X days..." — Time lost = real pain
- "I made a custom script..." — Cobbled solution = unmet need
- "I abandoned the project..." — Critical impact = urgency

### Negative Signals (Pivot or Stop)
- "It rarely happens to me" — Not frequent enough
- "[Tool X] is enough for me" — Not painful enough
- "Cool project!" without story — Politeness, not validation
